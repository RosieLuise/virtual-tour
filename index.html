Olympia Typewriter

Circa 1930


This "Olympia Model 7" typewriter (German: Schreibmaschine) was manufactured in 1930 by the German company Europa Schreibmaschinen AG.

Beer Stein

"The person who drinks beer sleeps well. Whoever sleeps well does not sin." This cup's rhyming message encourages beer drinking as a healthy and moral pastime.

Typewriter
