Passport & Luggage Tag

1951

Alwine Juretzko was born in Bottrop, Germany, in 1919. Her Reisepass brought her to New York City in 1954. The abbreviation "ORD" stands for O'Hare Airport, which was newly opened at the time of Alwine's arrival.
